Connecting you to your students and your students to employers

The WCS Portal provides careers services with their own private portal which not only enables them to manage their careers service, but also creates an engaged community of students and employers.

Learn More

Manage your Community

The careers service can manage its community and employer/student engagement, view student profiles/CVs and create ad hoc reports such as how many of my graduates are in employment.

Manage your Services

Students can connect with their cohorts, learn how to build a CV, be found by employers for internships, placements and graduate jobs and make appointments to see careers advisors.

Empower Employers

Employers can build a company profile, use the powerful search tool to engage with specific students, post job opportunities, book/manage events and invite specific students to attend.
